    • Welcome to UC Socially Dynamic!

    Our goal is to spread the idea that UCSD stands for UC Socially Dynamic: A concept that means UCSD is an engaging and exciting place to attend. We also wish to create an all-inclusive community by focusing on everyday students.

    There needs to be attention brought to all student since every single one makes up the collective culture!

    As seen on the right, #UCSociallyDynamic has been used to spread positivity and that we have a lively campus with various interesting characters from all walks of life. Our team personally went out and asked people whether they thought UCSD was socially dynamic or not. The people who agreed wrote their favorite memories or experiences, and th others put up possible improvements!
